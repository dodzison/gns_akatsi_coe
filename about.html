Brief profile

I am a 30-year-old Ghanaian computer scientist. I hold a Master of Science degree in computer science from University of Ghana, Legon-Accra. Currently, I am a Front-End Developer at Codeways. My works cut across network installations, web designs and application development using some of the popular programming languages and frameworks of recent time.

I am also a tutor at Akatsi College of Education, where I started work as national service personnel in 2012. During the past two years, I have taught Information Literacy Skills both at the College of Distance Education (CoDE) - UCC and at Institute of Education (IoE) - UCC at the Akatsi and Presbyterian College Centres. Other courses that I have taught are; Introduction to Information Technology, Number and basic algebra, Geometry and trigonometry, Programming in basics, Statistics and probability among others.

Aside my passion for teaching, I have a strong desire to do research. Some of my research works are; An Efficient MRC Based RNS-to-Binary Converter for the {2^(2n+1)-1,2^2n,2^2n-1} Moduli Set and Effects of TCP and SCTP Flows on Video Streaming Over UDP in the Internet
